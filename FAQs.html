ORDER & DELIVERY

How Do I Keep Track Of My Order?

You can track your order with the help of a link that you will receive in your order confirmation email/SMS.

Also, you can click on ‘Your Orders’ option available in the My-Account Page on our website.

Can I Change The Products Once I Have Placed An Order?

Unfortunately, we cannot change the product once order is placed.

Can I Have A Copy Of The Order Invoice Sent To My Email Address?

Yes, an order invoice link will be sent to your email/SMS.

What Are The Steps To Place An Order?
  • 1. Visit our global search engine to find the product you're searching for.
  • 2. Click on the desired product to view more details about the product.
  • 3. Select your desired item and click “Add to Cart”.
  • 4. To view and edit your cart, select the “My Cart” option in the menu.
  • 5. Apply discount coupon if available
  • 6. If you wish to continue as a guest, please enter your email address and Delivery information to proceed.
  • 7. If you are a returning customer, please enter your Login details to proceed.
  • 8. If you are a new customer and wish to register, register with your contact details.
  • 9. Click your preferred payment method and click on proceed to pay option.
  • 10. Your order will be placed once payment is received by us.
I Have Not Received My Order, But My Order Status Is Showing Delivered. What Should I Do?

In case the order is not delivered, please contact our customer support for assistance.

Is It Safe To Buy From 263stores?

263stores offers highly secure website with advanced encryption systems for payment process to ensure complete safety and protection to our valuable customers.

What technologies does 263stores use?

263stores runs on HTTPS which is a more secure transfer protocol. All communications between your browser and the website are encrypted which means that all transactions are more secure. All personal information is protected by secured networks to ensure all customer info kept safe and confidential.

PRIVACY & GENERAL INFORMATION

What Are Cookies And Why Do I Need To Allow Them?

We use cookies on our website to collect visitor statistics, for advertising purposes and to enhance the user's experience of our website. We may update this information time and again as needed.

Can I withdraw my consent?

Yes. If you wish to withdraw your consent, you will need to delete the cookies stored on your device in your web browser settings. Furthermore, change your web browser settings to disable cookies in the future to prevent cookies from getting stored on your device.

How Can I Contact Customer Care?

You can give us a call, contact us by email or live chat any time using the details provided on the 'Contact us' page.

What Measures Do We Take To Prevent Fraud?

All products are delivered to customers by using the online invoice generated upon successful payment.

All delivery addresses are recorded in the order history and verified by our respective departments before delivery.

If the system flags any transaction as fraudulent, we verify this by analysing all parameters used to detect fraud. In case of doubt, the transaction will be cancelled.

Where Can I Upload Images Of The Damaged Or Incorrect Products That Were Delivered To Me?

The customer needs to contact our support team to report an issue regarding the damaged, defective, or wrong product. A link will be provided to the customer on the registered email address after the customer support team has been contacted.

On Which Conditions Are Returns Applicable?

Only wrong, damaged, defective product(s) or product(s) with missing parts can be returned.

What Are The Criteria To Be Met For Products To Be Eligible For Returns?
  • 1. The customer must contact us within 7 days of the delivery.
  • 2. The product should be in an unused and resalable condition.
  • 3. The product should be in its original packaging including the brand
  • 4. The product must be returned by the customer in its entirety with all accompanying accessories or free gifts present therein.
Under Which Product Conditions Are Not Eligible For A Return?
  • 1. Specific categories like innerwear, lingerie, swimwear, beauty products, perfumes/deodorant, and clothing freebies, grocery & gourmet, jewellery, pet supplies, music, movies, batteries, etc., are not eligible for return and refund.
  • 2. Products with missing labels or accessories.
  • 3. Digital products.
  • 4. Products that have been tampered with or have missing serial numbers.
  • 5. A product that has been used or installed by the customer.
  • 6. Any product not in its original form or packaging.
  • 7. Refurbished products or pre-owned products are not eligible for returns.
  • 8. Products that are not damaged, defective, or different from what was originally ordered.
How Do I Issue An Intellectual Property Complaint?
  • 1. Report your complaint in writing and identify any relevant registration/application numbers, the registration country and the owner.
  • 2. Provide complete contact information (full name, postal address, email address and phone number).
  • 3. Provide a description of the content on our site that you claim infringes an IP right and describe it sufficiently to permit us to locate the material on our website (e.g. URLs and screenshots).
  • 4. Give a description of how the IP right is allegedly infringed.
  • 5. Provide an electronic or physical signature confirming you act on behalf of the IP right owner and
  • 6. Attach files relevant to your request.
