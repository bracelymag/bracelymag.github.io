  • Our Story

  • Zimbabwe has a problem

    Deep within the roots of Africa, there is a country called Zimbabwe. Everything is fine but there is one challenge. People want to check native products online before going for the sale. Also some want to setup an online store to market their products. But they don't know where to go.

  • Ideation

    One evening, two young friends were discussing on how they can add value to the country. Determined, they researched on community problems and brainstormed on the solutions. After some serious head-cracking, they came to a conclusion, that 263stores was what Zimbabweans need.

  • Joining the cause

    The two then shared their ideas with their friends and whilst others laughed at the idea, some were willing to take that risk. With the assembled team, this platform was then created to meet the demands of society.


  • After the launch

  • Our first user

    Within 2 hours 46 minutes, after the site launched, we got our first user. A pleasant surprise to us all.

  • Our first vendor

    A few minutes later, someone was just browsing for ecommerce solutions until they stumbled to our page. They got very curious, and wanted to test it out. They set up an online store to sell clothes and wearables. Alas, that was our first vendor.

  • Hype

    After some time, more users heard about us and the wonderful things that were done by our site and decided to join us.
