Our Story

Spearheading the ship of easy student accomodation search, three college freshmen dreamnt of a college setup with efficient and much appreciative us of technology. Meeting at the biggest institution in the country, UZ, the three namely, Tatenda Gwisayi, Courage Hwiridza and Shaun Nyararai were perplexed about how they can put off campus accomodation at the fingertips of the students.

Equality is the dream of the founders. In executing their dream, they wish to also give solutions to students coming from remote areas and those from outside Harare who did not get raise from the institution they wanted.

It was witnessed from statistics that universities in Zimbabwe can only house about a meagre 20% of the students on the campus. The other 80% would have to rely on off campus accomodation, residence of origin and on the other end, some dubious ways to stay on campus. There is no perfect flow information about off campus accomodation. No one really knows about the best renting places as they are rarely advertised or made known.

StudentsIN is a way to make such information known to students. Our platform enables everyone to see the houses available, chose a place of interest and apply to rent there. Our platform gives places basing on a first come, first save algorithm. We do this to avoid issues of favouritism, thus upholding our 'equality' principle. Our platform is aimed for both the landlords and the learners.
